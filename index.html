Minh-Nhut Dang

Hi, I’m Nhut, a senior Computer Science student in the Advanced Program at the University of Science, VNU-HCM.

I am currently completing my thesis at the Center for Computational Linguistics (CLC), focusing on the restoration of ancient Vietnamese steles from degraded data sources.

Additionally, I am seeking PhD opportunities to continue exploring the potential of multimodal language models in preserving traditional cultural values.

Research Interests

  • Natural Language Processing
  • Vision-Language Models
  • Multimodal and Context-Aware Learning
  • Historical Document Restoration
